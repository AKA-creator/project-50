Interesting Facts about Chameleons

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  2. Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  3. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting Facts about Camels

  1. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes
  2. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat
  3. Camels are very strong and can carry up to 900 pounds for 25 miles a day

Interesting Facts about Pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Interesting Facts about Bears

  1. Bears eat mostly meat and fish, but some bears also eat plants and insects
  2. Black bears can run at the speed up to 35mph
  3. The Asiatic black bear has the largest ears than other species of bears

Interesting Facts about pandas

  1. Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim
  2. An adult panda can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day
  3. Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown)

Interesting Facts about Cicadas

  1. They thrive in warm environments like the tropics
  2. Some cicadas could explode with fungus
  3. Cicadas have five eyes
